# AGENTS.md

> Keep this file concise. Update it whenever scope or design shifts — stale guidance hurts more than no guidance.

## What this is

Alper Ortac's personal site. A personal-brand surface, not a recruiter funnel — LinkedIn handles job context and is the most prominent entry in the sky layer.

## The shape

A vertical scroll journey through atmosphere, time, and depth.

- **Sky / day (top)** — linktree. Social and platform links drift as clouds. LinkedIn anchors as the sun.
- **Ground / dusk → night (middle)** — career, freelancing, side projects. Tangible objects in a softly abstract natural setting; *feel*, not literal.
- **Underground (bottom)** — deeper writing, insights, content snippets, opinions. Easter eggs mostly live here.

## Feel

- Nature-themed but abstract. Atmosphere over realism, no specific place.
- First-person cursor. No avatar, no cutscenes.
- Simple, whimsical animations.
- Voice is neutral (not first-person journal).
- Game-narrative and gamification are encouraged — they reflect Alper's personality.

## Mechanics

- **Scroll** drives the day → night transition and the descent.
- **Progress bar** always visible. Linear 0 → 80%. After 80% it visibly slows — depth requires effort, not just scrolling. Honest-liar pattern: completionists can push past where they got last time.
- **Digging** is a difficulty curve, easiest at the top of the dig zone:
  drag-clear → click-chip → hold-to-dig → tool-gated easter eggs.
- **Persistence** via localStorage — track found items and easter eggs across sessions.
- **Audio** is ambient. Default off, clear toggle.

## Constraints

- Mobile must work. Every desktop interaction needs a touch equivalent.
- Reduced-motion preference is respected.
