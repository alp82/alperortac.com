Hero subtitle typography — on the real sky-blue background

Every card sits on the site's real top-of-scroll background, the daytime sky #87CEEB (SKY_NOON, the exact color the shipped subtitle renders on at scroll 0). No switcher — this is the one true background. Both cycling words animate live with the real sequentialCycle logic. Find a favorite, click its “Copy spec”, and paste the line back into chat.

Background is locked to rgb(135,206,235) = #87CEEB. Designs read as dark elements for contrast; any light text sits on its own dark block (code-pills, the terminal window, knockout/bar slabs). Overflow is controlled by sizing: each cycling word's font-size is capped via clamp() so the longest pool phrases — “clever architecture” (Part 2) and “genuinely useful” (Part 4) — fit on one line in the narrowest column, with overflow:hidden on each card as a net.
